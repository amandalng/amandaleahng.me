Life so far –

I'm a pseudo third culture kid from Jakarta, currently living in mostly-sunny Singapore.
After graduating from Berkeley with a degree in Economics and Global Poverty, I kicked off my career as a management consultant and entrepreneur, building my passion project turned education company, Origo Edu.
These experiences informed and grew a relentless passion for design, technology, and social impact, and I quickly learned full-stack web dev (thanks, Le Wagon!), began some side hustles (Cafe Collective, Quick Favor Club), and ultimately landed my current Digital Product role at AMAAN, a Jakarta-based digital platform working to financially include and empower poor women across rural Indonesia.
I'm constantly looking for opportunities to build cool things that help those around me – responsibly, inclusively, and impactfully.
If any of what I just described is up your alley, hit me up!
